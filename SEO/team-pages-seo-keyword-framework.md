## Core Keyword Rule

Each team page gets one page-level core keyword:

```text
{Team} World Cup 2026 Schedule
```

Examples:

```text
Mexico World Cup 2026 Schedule
United States World Cup 2026 Schedule
Canada World Cup 2026 Schedule
Argentina World Cup 2026 Schedule
Brazil World Cup 2026 Schedule
```

The readable core keyword should appear naturally in:

```text
Title
Meta description
H1
At least one H2
One early paragraph
Relevant internal-link anchor text
FAQ where natural
```

Do not force the exact phrase into every heading or paragraph. The target density remains 3%-5% for the keyword family, not repeated exact-match stuffing.

```text

```

Do not solve the 48-page risk by adding the same paragraph block to every team page.
