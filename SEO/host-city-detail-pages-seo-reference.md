Purpose:

```text
Each city page should answer a local planning question: which World Cup 2026 matches are in this host city, what dates and stages are involved, which teams appear, which stadium is used, and what should a fan check before travel or ticket decisions.
```

```text

```

SEO and GEO guidance:

```text
Do not convert the city pages back into a generic template.
Keep the H1/title city-specific.
Keep the first-screen copy focused on the actual city value: final host, opening match, semifinal route, third-place match, high-volume base, knockout route, Canada/Mexico route or regional travel route.
Use "World Cup 2026 schedule {city}", "{city} World Cup 2026 matches", "{city} stadium", "{city} fixtures" and related wording naturally.
Avoid repeating "host city" in every sentence.
Keep official-source reminders visible because travel, ticket and stadium details can change.
```
