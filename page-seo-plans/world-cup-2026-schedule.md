## 1. Search Intent

Users want the complete World Cup 2026 match schedule by date, team, tournament stage, host city, stadium and kickoff time. The page must help users quickly answer:

- When does World Cup 2026 start and end?
- What matches are on each date?
- Which teams play in each fixture?
- Which city and stadium host each match?
- What is the kickoff time?
- Can I filter by team, date, stage or city?
- Where can I download the schedule as PDF, Excel or CSV?
